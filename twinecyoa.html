Strung out Thoughts

This project was very fun and remains a work-in-progress. It is a survival choose your own adventure style game about maintaining sanity, hunger, and sleep through the stresses of life in the modern information age. You, a college transfer student, have to navigate a clerical error that is preventing you from applying and you have one week until the deadline. The twist is that, if your sanity drops, your text options get weirder and it becomes harder to bring your sanity back up.

Face the perils of everyday poor college kid life with just as much stress and no payoff!
