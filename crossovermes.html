Crossover Mes or Detritus vs. Neatness


Digital Video, 2018. This is a combination of a multi-channeled video piece. These two videos are intended to be played simultaneously across the room from each other as part of an installation piece. The first half of the audio is meant for the images on the left and the second half of the audio is for the images on the right. It is a statement about how the two opposing ways of life are both equally valid forms of happiness.
