	This is my Project 2. I think it is much better since I got some code running
in the draw loop creating the line in the background and another method added to the class.
My methods are jcDisplay, jcUpdate, jcWiggle, and jcchangeColor. jcDisplay draws and ellipse
with a black outline, and fills it in. jcUpdate moves where the circle is drawn downwards
and to the right with wraparound logic. jcWiggle changes where the circle is drawn by moving
it randomly either -2 or 2 pixels. jcchangeColor changes the colors of the circles based on
the millis() command, but in this version I had to do it with frameCount() since millis()
won't wait if the program is lagging, which is the case when saveFrame is active. The
aesthetic effect reminds me of the art from the cover of a prog-rock concept album.
