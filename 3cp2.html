Untitled (Watch), 2020, 3D render, 3D print.

Untitled (Watch)

Iâ€™ve long been interested in the art of watchmaking and I am an amateur watch collector and tinkerer. This piece is a new case, caseback, and dial, designed for use with a Citizen Quartz movement caliber 2034 and all other cheap quartz movement clones. The rendered images are to scale with the requirements of that movement. The dial is very thin, and the lugs are 18mm apart which is a common strap width for watches.

I chose a small size and a small and cheap movement because this watch is designed for me and I have small wrists. It performs a function that no watches yet perform, by showing not hours, minutes, and seconds left in the day, but only showing time elapsed since the watch was started. The watch dial I have modeled has small raised indents that are section off a quarter of the dial to denote not the time, but the amount of time spent. It is based on a discipline technique/work philosophy I practice where, for every 45 minutes spent working, 15 is to be spent resting.
