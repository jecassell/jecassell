Jacob Cassell

works with 3D rendering and scanning software and 3D printers, as well as outdated electronic media to explore issues on point of view and mental health. He seeks to help people to question their own viewpoint on a variety of topics and their own understanding of mental health. He seeks to do this by using New Media Art to make you think more deeply about every day objects and familiar and retired media.
