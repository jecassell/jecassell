Coca-Cola Ray Gun

3D Render, 3D Print, Acrylic Paint on ABS Plastic, 2018. This is a work made out of free pre-made 3D rendered objects. Combined is a Coca-Cola bottle, Coca-Cola cans, and an M1911 pistol. The piece is a statement about violent advertising.
