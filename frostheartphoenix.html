Frostheart Phoenix

This Frostheat Phoenix is part of a series of game pieces created to be used for a tabletop game of my own design. This series is called "Where I came From." This is a sculpt from Citadel's Games Workshop for their Warhammer fantasy miniatures line. It was was painted over the course of three days as a gift primarily using a drybrush technique. This piece was made in early 2016.
