Cadre Visual Novel, 2020, Interactive Ren.py Software

Cadre Visual Novel

Click here or on any of the images to access the free download link on my itch.io page.

This piece of interactive software is part of an ongoing project that fellow digital artist, Eric Lamothe, and I are working on together. The project is to maintain a sense of community and friendship among the members of CADRE S.O., and those who are CADRE adjacent. We mentioned to Professor Durie that we wanted to make a visual novel about our friends and acquaintences and that we wanted to get the word out through the CADRE channels and he loved the project so much, that he greenlit the piece immediately and even provided all of the photos of the art building for us. The project took approximately a week to produce.

It was coded with Python through Ren.py and the images of our friends were provided through those friends. Any semblance to any persons living or dead is entirely coincidental.
