Cameras in Use

	This video is an art piece that incorporates two themes. The first theme is that of a drunken memory, in that you can only
   rmember certain vivid parts but the rest is just visual noise. The other is a commentary on people who vlog in public and just
   show the things that interest them. They try to create a spectacle for likes. This is about cameras in use in public and the
   overabundance of visual noise.
